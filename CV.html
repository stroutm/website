Hi! I'm Sara and I love finding new ways to bring together math and water systems.

I am a Ph.D. student in Civil and Environmental Engineering at the University of Michigan, advised by Dr. Branko Kerkez and Dr. Nancy G. Love.

I am passionate about urban water systems and tackle problems in this field from a systems-level perspective, integrating data-driven modeling and control. My aim in doing this is to develop systems-level decision-making tools focused around real-time control and operation.

My research is focused on signal processing, data-driven modeling, and control in stormwater and wastewater systems, but in a broader sense I am interested in elucidating and impacting the role of data in decisions made around water systems, informing control operations and risk evaluation.
