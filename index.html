Hi! I'm Sara and I love finding new ways to bring together math and water systems.

I am a Ph.D. student in Civil and Environmental Engineering at the University of Michigan, advised by Dr. Branko Kerkez and Dr. Nancy G. Love.

My research focuses on coupling environmental and water resources engineering, data science and machine learning, and systems analysis to tackle problems related to competing water quantity and quality objectives and the role of data in decision actions.
